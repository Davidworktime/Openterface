## KeyMod Series

Transform your phone or tablet into a **universal input device** for any computer. The KeyMod app connects to the KeyMod hardware via USB-C or Bluetooth LE, sending keystrokes, mouse movements, and gamepad inputs to the target.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   ### :fontawesome-brands-android:{ .lg } **Android**

    ***

    KeyMod controller app with keyboard, trackpad, gamepad, macros, and voice input.

    [:octicons-download-24: Download latest APK](https://github.com/TechxArtisanStudio/Openterface_KeyMod_Android/tags) <br>
    [:octicons-mark-github-16: Openterface_KeyMod_Android](https://github.com/TechxArtisanStudio/Openterface_KeyMod_Android)

-   ### :fontawesome-brands-apple:{ .lg } **iOS / iPadOS**
