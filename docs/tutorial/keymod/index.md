## What Is KeyMod?

KeyMod is a **keyboard and mouse emulator** — a USB device that sits between your peripherals (keyboard, mouse) and either your host computer or a target device. With a single toggle, you can switch your keyboard and mouse between two machines — no extra USB hub or software KVM needed.

Unlike KVM devices, KeyMod does **not** capture video. It focuses purely on reliable HID (Human Interface Device) emulation, making it ideal for:

- Switching your favorite mechanical keyboard between a workstation and a server/Raspberry Pi
- Using a single mouse and keyboard across a desktop PC and a laptop
- Headless device management where video is handled separately
